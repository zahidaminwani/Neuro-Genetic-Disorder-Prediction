Diet Plan

  • Foods rich in omega-3 (fish, flaxseeds, walnuts).
  • Maintain hydration and balanced nutrition.
  • Avoid artificial sweeteners and processed foods.
  • Monitor blood sugar levels, as fluctuations can trigger seizures.

Medications
