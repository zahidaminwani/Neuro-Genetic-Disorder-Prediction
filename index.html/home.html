Neurogenetic Disease Detection

Utilizing AI and advanced machine learning models to predict and detect neurogenetic diseases.

Key Points :-

Huntington’s Disease:

A genetic neurodegenerative disorder caused by a mutation in the HTT gene, leading to progressive motor dysfunction, cognitive decline, and psychiatric symptoms.

Epilepsy

A neurological disorder characterized by recurrent, unprovoked seizures due to abnormal electrical activity in the brain.

Alzheimer’s Disease

A progressive neurodegenerative disorder marked by memory loss, cognitive impairment, and behavioral changes, primarily due to the accumulation of amyloid plaques and tau tangles in the brain.

Parkinson’s Disease

A neurodegenerative disorder caused by the loss of dopamine-producing neurons in the substantia nigra, leading to tremors, rigidity, bradykinesia, and postural instability.

About Us

We are Arham Chowdary and Zahid Wani, passionate researchers and developers dedicated to advancing medical technology. Our project, Neurogenetic, is an innovative neurogenetic disorder prediction system designed to assist in early diagnosis and risk assessment. Combining expertise in machine learning, genetics, and healthcare, we aim to provide an efficient and accessible tool for identifying conditions like Huntington’s, Alzheimer’s, and Parkinson’s disease. Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and medicine, empowering healthcare professionals and patients with reliable predictive insights.
