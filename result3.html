Precautionary Measures

  • Genetic counseling for individuals with a family history.
  • Physical therapy to maintain mobility and prevent falls.
  • Cognitive exercises to slow mental decline.
  • Speech and swallowing therapy for communication and eating issues.
  • Psychological support (counseling, stress management techniques).

Diet Plan

  • High-calorie diet (due to high energy expenditure from involuntary movements).
  • Healthy fats (avocados, nuts, olive oil) for brain health.
  • Protein-rich foods (eggs, lean meat) for muscle maintenance.
  • Easy-to-swallow foods (smoothies, soups, mashed foods).
  • Avoid caffeine, alcohol, and processed foods that worsen symptoms.

Medications

  • Tetrabenazine, Deutetrabenazine to control involuntary movements.
  • Antipsychotics (Olanzapine, Risperidone) for mood swings and aggression.
  • Antidepressants (SSRIs like Fluoxetine) for depression and anxiety.
  • Muscle relaxants (Baclofen) for muscle rigidity.
